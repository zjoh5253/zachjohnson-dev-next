D2D Blitz is a purpose-built CRM for door-to-door sales teams — the kind of workflow that generic tools like HubSpot weren't designed for. Reps work fast, in the field, with no time to navigate complex dashboards. The platform is optimized around speed: log a knock, track a disposition, schedule a callback, and move to the next door. Built with Next.js and TypeScript, the UI stays out of the way so reps can focus on the conversation, not the software.

On the automation side, D2D Blitz handles follow-up sequences, territory assignment, and team-level performance tracking without manual input from managers. Leads that don't close on the first visit get queued for follow-up at configurable intervals, reducing the chance that a warm prospect goes cold because nobody remembered to call back. The result is a tighter feedback loop between field activity and pipeline visibility — giving sales leadership the data they need without adding friction for the reps generating it.
