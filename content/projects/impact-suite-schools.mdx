Impact Suite for Schools is a comprehensive K-12 safety platform that consolidates compliance management, threat assessment, student mental health monitoring, and emergency response into a single system. The product came out of a strategic pivot — recognizing that school districts were stitching together four or five disconnected tools to handle student safety workflows, and that an integrated platform would be both a better product and a stronger business. The bet paid off: within five months of launch, the platform was live in 20 school districts and generating $300K in ARR, with $3M in active pipeline.

## Built Fast with a Small Team

The entire platform was built by a two-person development team in five months — a timeline that wouldn't have been possible without leaning heavily on AI-assisted development tools throughout the process. Rather than slowing down to build every feature from scratch, the team used AI to accelerate boilerplate, draft data models, generate test coverage, and move through the compliance-heavy requirements quickly. The result was a production-ready, district-deployable platform in a timeframe that previously would have required a team three or four times the size.

## What the Platform Does

The platform unifies four previously siloed workflows: compliance tracking to ensure districts meet state and federal safety requirements, structured threat assessment workflows that guide administrators through evidence-based decision trees, student mental health monitoring with flagging and case management, and emergency response coordination for lockdowns, evacuations, and critical incidents. Bringing these into one system means administrators work from a single dashboard, data flows between modules automatically, and nothing falls through the cracks during high-stress situations.

## Results

Twenty districts signed on within the first five months of availability — a strong signal that the integrated approach resonated with administrators tired of managing disconnected tools. The $300K ARR baseline and $3M pipeline position Impact Suite for Schools as a serious player in the EdTech safety space, with room to expand into additional districts and state markets as compliance requirements continue to tighten across the country.
